About Me

Hi, my name is Charles Sabarillo and I live in the city of Marikina in Metro Manila, Philippines . I graduated from CIIT College of Arts and Technology with a major of Game Development .

I love to code . Being a developer is my passion. I like to create and develop things that are useful, helpful or fun. I tend to think of ideas that makes the things that I and other people do easier and more efficient.

I also like to play some video games at times, so I guess I'm kinda a gamer. I have played Monster Hunter, Dota 2, Minecraft, and more. I'm also an active user of Discord , which is a text, voice and video chat app designed for gamers.

My other hobbies and interests include speed solving the Rubik's Cube, playing the guitar, talking with people in Discord, and learning Korean. I'm also a K-Pop fan!
